About Us

Welcome to Tim's Heavenly Dumplings, located at 200 50th Street, New York, NY. As a family-owned restaurant, we bring the rich culinary traditions of Shanghai to New York City with passion and dedication. Our menu celebrates the distinct flavors of Shanghai, crafted with the finest ingredients and meticulous attention to detail. Each dish bursts with authentic taste, offering a delightful culinary experience. At Tim's Heavenly Dumplings, guests are welcomed as family. It's a place where stories are shared and every meal becomes a flavorful journey. Come and enjoy the authentic flavors and warm hospitality that define our restaurant.
